IBA Faith Statement

We believe that God, the creator of heaven and earth and the father of all, Jesus, the son of God, Christ our savior and Lord, and the Holy Spirit and its work. We believe that Jesus died on the cross for the forgiveness of our sins and Jesus will return as a bridegroom to marry us and we as Christians must prepare ourselves as his brides to marry him. Thus, we focus heavily on the prophecy and fulfillment of the New Testament - Revelation to edify each other and to prepare for the day.
